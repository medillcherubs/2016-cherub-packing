Backpack

Keep your backpack organized and always be ready to report.

Notebook/Folder

Lanyard

Keep your keys with you at all times while running in and out of CRC.

Notebook

Always take notes on your subjects and their surroundings during interviews.

Camera

Remember to take photos to go with your stories and document events.

Recorder

Record your interviews for accuracy, but make sure you ask permission first.

Pens and Pencils

Your favorite writing tool will help you write fast during interviews.

Headphones

Name tag

Use your name tag as your press pass. It identifies you as a Northwestern student.

Dorm Necessities

Back
Next

Snacks

Snacks are a must during and after a long day of writing and interviewing.

Detergent Pods

Detergent pods make laundry as simple as putting yout laundry in the machine, throwing in a pod and pressing a button.

Laundry Bag

A big bag allows you to transport your laundry downstairs to the laundry room and back up.

Shower Shoes

Waterproof shoes are a must when showering so your clean feet don't touch the floor.

Shower Caddy

A caddy allows for easy transport to and from the shower without dropping anything.

Hangers

Hang your clothes in the closet fresh out of the dryer to avoid wrinkles.

Next

Small bag

A small bag allows you to avoid checking your bag on field trips and is light and easy to carry.

Water bottle

Water will help you stay hydrated in the Chicago heat.

Cell phone

Your phone will help you navigate and communicate with friends on and off campus.

Wallet

Keep your money and credit cards organized and handy in a wallet.

Sunglasses

Shield your eyes from the sun when you go into town with sunglasses.

Umbrella

An umbrella is a must so Chicago's unpredictable weather won't leave you stranded in the rain.
